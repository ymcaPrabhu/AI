OFFICE MEMORANDUM

Subject: Extension of tenure of Shri Prabhu Narayan, Director (Cyber Security), DEA.

The undersigned is directed to state that the competent authority has approved
the extension of deputation of Shri Prabhu Narayan, Director (Cyber Security),
Department of Economic Affairs, beyond 30.12.2025, for a period of one year,
i.e., up to 30.12.2026, within the permissible five-year limit for lateral entrants.

This issues with the approval of the Finance Minister.

(Under Secretary)
